ME210 COMPETITION

Reflection

What Went Well

Overall the robot performed reliably in competition and our core systems held up under pressure.

Challenges

Weight Distribution

Balancing the weight between the two chassis levels proved difficult and affected driving performance.

IR Beacon Sensing

Reliably detecting the IR beacon across the competition floor required significant tuning and was a persistent challenge throughout development.

Wire Management

Securing boards and wires was an area where we fell short. During competition, two wires came loose:

  • Ground wire to the stepper motor
  • Wire to one of the drive wheel motors

Additionally, the Arduino Mega board was not mounted to the chassis, leaving it unsecured during the run.

What We Learned

Embrace Design Iteration

We went through many design iterations across subsystems. A key takeaway was to not be scared to let go of a design — moving on quickly when something isn't working is more valuable than over-investing in a flawed approach.
